How It Works

Discover our comprehensive approach to inventory management

Our system revolutionizes inventory management through a seamless, integrated approach. Starting with barcode scanning technology, we efficiently capture and digitize your inventory data, ensuring accurate and real-time stock tracking.

The heart of Fundo-ISMS lies in its AI-powered analytics engine. This sophisticated tool processes your inventory data to generate comprehensive statistics and predictive insights. It forecasts potential stock shortages, identifies product trends, and provides actionable recommendations for inventory optimization.

For financial compliance and business intelligence, our system maintains detailed transaction records. Every movement, sale, and adjustment is meticulously logged, creating an audit-ready trail that helps you maintain accountability and make informed decisions.

The order management module streamlines your supply chain operations. Track orders in real-time, manage supplier relationships, and maintain optimal stock levels through automated reordering suggestions. Our system provides complete visibility of your order lifecycle from placement to delivery.

Complete your business operations with our integrated Point of Sale (POS) system. This optional module transforms your inventory management system into a comprehensive retail solution, enabling direct sales, inventory synchronization, and marketing capabilities all in one platform.
